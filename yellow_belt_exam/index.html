Helping others with your computer

A computer is a vital tool for anyone living in this day and age. This is why we aim to donate every 1 in 10 computers we collect to charitable and worthy causes.

Safe and Secure

We ensure all peviously stored information on any of the systems we collect is either removed, given to you or destroyed.

Eco-Friendly

We aim to reuse all that we collect from you. Reducing wastage and the manufacture of new systems has a range of benefit for our futures on this planet.

Helping Others

We donate 1 in 10 of the machines tho charities and worthy causes. This helps grow individuals who are in need of the benefits a computer can bring.

Why waste? We will collect...

The environmental and social benefits of reuse do not only help conserve natural resources, but help reduce air and water pollution caused by hazardous disposal. Reuse reduces the amount of greenhouse gas emissions caused by the manufacturing of new products. It simply makes good sense to let us collect and reuse your equipment.
